Stat 001: Section 101

Here is an equation, pertaining to the probability that a random variable \(X\) will be between 0 and 1. $$ \mathbb{P}(X \in [0, 1]) = \int_{0}^{1} f_X(x) \ \mathrm{d}x $$ There are also some additional formulas here:
  1. \(\displaystyle \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} (-1)^k \frac{x^k}{k!} = e^{-x}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty} \left( 1 + \frac{x}{n} \right) = e^{x} \)
